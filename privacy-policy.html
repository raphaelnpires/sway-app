1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account on Sway, we collect the following information:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account communications
  • Username — your chosen display name within the app
  • Authentication credentials — managed securely through Firebase Authentication (email/password or Sign in with Apple)
  • Profile information — including any optional bio, profile photo, or display name you provide

Content Preferences

Sway is a content discovery platform focused on aesthetic and sensual (non-explicit) media. To personalize your experience, we collect:

  • Interest tags — content categories you select (e.g., "teasing," "stockings," "leather," and similar kink-adjacent topics)
  • Intensity preference — your chosen content intensity level (Easy, Medium, or Bold)
  • Engagement data — content you like, save, or interact with
  • Creator follows — which creators you follow within the app

Creator Information

If you register as a Creator, we additionally collect:

  • Content you upload to the platform
  • Subscription pricing you set for VIP access
  • Revenue and payout information related to the 80/20 revenue share

Payment Information

Payment details (credit card numbers, bank account information) are collected and processed exclusively by Stripe, our third-party payment processor. We do not store your full payment card details on our servers. See Section 3 for details.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash logs and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • General usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service — operate your account, authenticate your identity, and deliver core app functionality
  • Personalize your feed — use your selected interest tags and intensity preferences to curate content relevant to your tastes
  • Process transactions — facilitate subscriptions, tips, and creator payouts through Stripe
  • Improve the app — analyze crash reports and usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance performance
  • Communicate with you — send account-related notifications, security alerts, and (with your consent) promotional updates
  • Enforce our policies — ensure compliance with our Terms of Service, community guidelines, and content standards
  • Ensure safety — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our policies

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your content preferences and interest tags are used solely to improve your in-app experience.

3. Payment Processing

Sway uses Stripe as its sole payment processor for all financial transactions, including:

  • Monthly VIP subscriptions to creators
  • Tips and one-time payments to creators
  • Creator payout processing

When you make a purchase, your payment information is transmitted directly to Stripe and is subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy. We receive from Stripe only a limited set of transaction data, including:

  • Transaction amount and status
  • Last four digits of the payment card (for display and support purposes)
  • Stripe customer and transaction identifiers

Revenue Share

Sway operates on an 80/20 revenue split. Creators receive 80% of subscription and tip revenue, and the platform retains 20%. Payout records are maintained for accounting and tax compliance purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored and managed using Google Firebase services, specifically:

  • Firebase Authentication — securely manages user sign-in via email/password and Sign in with Apple
  • Cloud Firestore — stores user profiles, content metadata, preferences, follows, and other application data

Firebase infrastructure is hosted on Google Cloud Platform, which maintains:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • SOC 1, SOC 2, and SOC 3 compliance
  • ISO 27001 certification
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

While we implement commercially reasonable security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Crash Reporting

Sway uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information. This helps us identify and resolve technical issues. Crashlytics may collect:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Crash stack traces and error logs
  • Crashlytics installation UUID (a unique, anonymous identifier for each app install)
  • General performance metrics

Crashlytics data is used exclusively for diagnosing and fixing technical issues. It does not include your personal content preferences, payment information, or account credentials.

7. Your Rights

General Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Export your personal data in a portable format

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

European Economic Area Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

Our lawful bases for processing your data include: consent (for content preferences), contract performance (for providing the service), and legitimate interests (for security and service improvement).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@swayapp.com. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days (or 45 days for complex requests, with notice).

8. Children's Privacy

Sway is strictly for users aged 18 and older.

Sway contains content intended for adult audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. Users are required to confirm they are at least 18 years old during account registration.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a person under 18, we will take immediate steps to delete that information and terminate the associated account. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@swayapp.com.

9. Content Policy

Sway is a platform for aesthetic, sensual, and kink-focused content discovery. All content on the platform must comply with our Community Guidelines and Content Policy:

  • Non-explicit content only — Sway does not permit sexually explicit or pornographic material
  • Consent-focused — all content must depict consensual scenarios and all individuals must be 18+
  • Creator responsibility — creators are responsible for ensuring their content complies with applicable laws and our guidelines
  • Content categorization — creators must accurately tag their content with appropriate interest tags and intensity levels
  • Prohibited content — content depicting minors, non-consensual acts, extreme violence, illegal activity, or any content violating applicable law is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination

We reserve the right to remove content and suspend or terminate accounts that violate these policies at our sole discretion. Users may report content that violates our guidelines through in-app reporting mechanisms.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Active Accounts

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active and as needed to provide you with our services.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time. Upon account deletion:

  • Your profile information, preferences, and engagement data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Content you uploaded as a Creator will be removed from public access
  • Your authentication credentials will be removed from Firebase Authentication
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained (this data cannot identify you)

Financial Records

Transaction records and financial data may be retained for up to 7 years after account deletion to comply with tax, legal, and regulatory requirements. This includes payout records for creators and subscription transaction history.

Legal Obligations

We may retain certain data beyond the standard deletion period if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
